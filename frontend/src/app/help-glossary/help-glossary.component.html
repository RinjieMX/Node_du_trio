Glossary

French History

Eiffel Tower
A famous iron lattice landmark located in Paris, France.
Motto
A short phrase or slogan expressing the guiding principles or ideals of a group.
Bastille
A fortress and state prison in Paris, known for its storming on July 14, 1789, a turning point in the French Revolution.

Python

Statement
A unit of code that expresses an action to be carried out.
Python
A high-level, interpreted programming language known for its readability and versatility.
Features
Characteristics or functionalities offered by a programming language or software.
Variable
A named storage location capable of holding data that can be changed during program execution.

Europe Geography

Balkan Peninsula
A region in Southeast Europe known for its diverse cultures and landscapes.
Land of a Thousand Lakes
Refers to Finland, which is known for its numerous lakes.
Currency
The system of money used in a particular country or region.
Landlocked Countries
Countries that are entirely surrounded by land, with no coastline.

English

Greetings
Expressions or words used to welcome or acknowledge someone.
Synonym
A word or phrase that means the same or nearly the same as another word or phrase in the same language.
Plural Form
The grammatical form indicating more than one of something.
